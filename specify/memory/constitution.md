# Constitution — Designing Your Life with AI

## Purpose
This book guides readers to design their life and career using AI. It provides frameworks, exercises, and AI prompts for personal growth, experimentation, and habit design.

## Rules
1. All AI prompts should be used to generate actionable insights.
2. Chapters follow a sequential life-design process.
3. All experiments should be tracked and reflected upon.
4. Ethics, privacy, and long-term guardrails must always be considered.
5. Portfolio and micro-income experiments must be validated and iterated.

## Outputs
- Chapters with structured exercises
- AI prompt results
- Reflections and experiment logs
