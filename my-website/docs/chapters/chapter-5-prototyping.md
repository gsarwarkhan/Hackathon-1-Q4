# Chapter 5 — Rapid Prototyping: Test Experiments with AI

Now that you have ideas for life and career experiments, the next step is to test them quickly. Rapid prototyping allows you to validate ideas without committing too much time or resources.

**Topics covered:**
- Principles of rapid prototyping applied to life experiments  
- Using AI to simulate outcomes or plan small tests  
- Measuring results and learning quickly  
- Iterating experiments based on feedback  

### Example Exercise:
1. Select one experiment from your ideation list.  
2. Define a 1–2 week “prototype” to test it with minimal commitment.  
3. Use AI to plan the steps, resources needed, and potential outcomes.  
4. Reflect on what worked, what didn’t, and how to improve it.  

**Reflection:**  
Rapid prototyping helps you avoid overthinking and accelerates learning, allowing you to refine your life design continuously.
